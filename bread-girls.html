BREAD GIRLS
Winter 2018 - Present

During a particularly caffeine-induced doodle session last holiday break, my friend Natalia and I created a series of characters representing different types of bread. We were surprised how well we could match personality archetypes to different types of bread. What started as a joke about our love of bread grew into the concept for a full dating simulator.
